Pepper Keychain

Pencil on Paper

The subject of the drawing is a keychain of red chili peppers. It is believed to be a symbol of good luck in Italy and hung to ward off evil. Usually gravity acts in a downward force, but I drew the keychain diagonally because I preferred the composition that way.
