About Me

Hi I'm Ashlie - a designer and artist from upstate New York! I am currently a first-year student attending Cornell University.

I have experience designing shirts, posters, and logos for clubs and local non-profit organizations, including the Best Buddies club, Ski club, and Homes for Orphaned Pets Exist (HOPE). I also have three years of experience making friendship bracelets and ran a small business, Elephabunnydiys, selling these bracelets with over 300 orders worldwide. My recent passion has been learning to create digital art, which I started doing over the summer!
